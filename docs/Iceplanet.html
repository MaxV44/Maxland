The planet Kindurn is a dark iceball far beyond the sun. Its dim surface is fogged and clouded by vast banks of slowly drifting ice crystals., with frequent storms making combat here difficult, but the exotic crystals found here keep many people coming here

Mission rule-Sun starved! It is ALWAYS quasi-night fighting. The line of sight limitation is 36 “ BS is -1 for all ranges above 12” No Leadership penalties. As in normal night fighting these penalties are not suffered by units with Night Vision.
