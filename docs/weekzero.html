Week zero. Mechanicum and Ultramarines Vs Orks!

Mechanicum and Ultramarine victory!

The first battle has just occurred in the Orgonergia system. A massive ork raid slammed into the outer hive districts. Warboss ShitSnacha’s spearhead almost got its stompa into the artillery park that was holding the Ork hordes at bay. An all out defense by the Ultramarines and Mechanicum was able to blast the stompa into scrap. The line was held. But why were the Orks so determined to suddenly break into the main hive’s stout defenses? The Orks were the first to arrive, but they were not unexpected.
